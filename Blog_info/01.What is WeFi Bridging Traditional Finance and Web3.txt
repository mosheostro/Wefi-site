# Introduction
WeFi is a fintech ecosystem designed to integrate traditional financial services with Web3 technologies. By establishing a framework known as a "Deobank," the project aims to facilitate the transition between legacy banking systems and decentralized finance (DeFi). As the demand for digital assets increases among global users, WeFi provides the infrastructure necessary to navigate both financial worlds within a single, compliant environment.

# What WeFi is
WeFi is a global financial ecosystem that combines the features of a neo-bank with the technical capabilities of blockchain protocols. It functions as a platform where users can manage traditional fiat currencies alongside digital assets. A core component of the system is its commitment to non-custodial solutions, ensuring that individuals maintain full control over their private keys and funds while accessing professional financial tools.

# How it connects TradFi and Web3
The platform acts as a bridge by embedding traditional finance (TradFi) instruments directly into blockchain infrastructure. This connection is achieved through specialized settlement layers that allow for the seamless movement of value between bank accounts and decentralized protocols. By incorporating regulatory compliance and identity verification (KYC) into its technological stack, WeFi enables users to interact with Web3 assets while remaining within a structured legal framework.

# Key value proposition
* **Integrated Banking Infrastructure:** Access to global bank accounts and payment cards that interact directly with digital asset balances.
* **Non-Custodial Security:** Users retain absolute ownership of their funds, reducing the counterparty risks associated with centralized exchanges.
* **Simplified User Experience:** A unified interface designed to make complex blockchain operations accessible to a broader audience.
* **Regulatory Compliance:** Built-in mechanisms to ensure transactions meet international financial standards and transparency requirements.

# Conclusion
WeFi provides a structured approach to reconciling the differences between traditional banking and the emerging Web3 economy. By focusing on non-custodial technology and regulatory alignment, the platform offers a practical path for users to utilize the benefits of blockchain without abandoning the security of established financial systems.
